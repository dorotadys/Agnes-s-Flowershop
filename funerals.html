Funerals

At a time of loss, flowers are both a mark of respect and a gesture of comfort. A tribute is an eloquent way to express your feelings and personally communicate your sympathy.

The designs on this website act as a guide and offer style suggestions that can be adapted using different flowers or colours, creating whatever message you wish to convey.

Please ask for a quotation if you would like a mixed floral casket arrangement. If you have any questions you would like to ask us about our sympathy flowers please do not hesitate to contact us, we will be happy to help.
